South Florida Market Guide
01 — Inventory

Luxury inventory is tight. Be pre-approved and ready to move — best properties go in 48 hours.

02 — Pricing

Waterfront commands a 30–50% premium. Palm Beach has seen 8–12% YoY appreciation over three years.

03 — Off-Market

Up to 40% of luxury deals happen off-market. A well-connected agent isn't optional — it's essential.

04 — Negotiation

Offer structure matters as much as price. Cash, flexible closing, clean contingencies win deals.

05 — Timing

Miami peaks Oct–Apr. Summer gives more leverage. Palm Beach's season creates distinct buying windows.

Neighborhood Guide
Miami Shores

Quiet, tree-lined streets and charming mid-century homes. A hidden gem for families seeking suburban calm with easy Miami access.

Coral Gables

Mediterranean architecture, top-rated schools, and the iconic Miracle Mile. One of Miami's most prestigious and livable neighborhoods.

Miami Beach

Iconic oceanfront living. Art Deco history, world-class dining, and some of the most recognizable addresses in Florida.

Brickell

Miami's financial district. Ultra-luxury high-rises, walkable amenities, and top restaurants. Best for urban professionals and investors.

Downtown Miami

The cultural core of the city. Arts, entertainment, and waterfront living — rapidly evolving and full of opportunity.

Midtown

Design District adjacent with a vibrant arts and dining scene. A favorite for creative professionals and young buyers.

Coconut Grove

Miami's oldest neighborhood. Lush, tree-lined streets, bayfront estates, and a bohemian-meets-luxury character unlike anywhere else.
